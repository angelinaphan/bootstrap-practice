Lala is a 1975 Sanrio character who is one of the Little Twin Stars; a pair of Sanrio characters who look like young angels. Lala was born in Omoiyari Star (also known as the Star of Compassion)[1] on December 24th (Christmas Eve) in the Yume Star-Cloud. She traveled a long way to Earth with her twin brother Kiki so that both could become "wonderful shining stars"
