twin stars
twin star is a character out of many other by sanrio. they were born on dec, 24. they are twin duo.
kiki
kiki is the younger brother with short blue hair. he wears a long sleeve white gown and carrys a flying star on his back.
lala
lala is the older sister with long pink hair. she also wears a long sleeve white gown and carrys a star wand with her.
